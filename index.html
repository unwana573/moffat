OUR STORY

The Black Ash was found in 1996 by Margret and Jonathan Ash. The ambitious farmer couple from Texas and knew early on what it meant to not only raise
cattle in a sustain and animal-friendly way but also to appreciate the taste of an excellent meat dish. Together they moved to California and changed the
resturant culture with the opening of black ash for good. Since then, guest have been experiencing what it means to eat high quality meat dishes in a rustic
and modern atmosphere.
